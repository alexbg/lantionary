What is Lantionary?

Lantionary is a desktop application that help you to learn new languages. Usually when we are reading a text in other language we don’t know some words or even don’t remember the grammar, so with Lantionary you will be able to save all that you learnt, find it quickly and control it. What are the features it will has?
  • Save the new words you have read.
  • Filter the worlds and grammar.
  • Save the new grammar you have learnt.
  • A dashboard to control the words or grammar you learnt, what are the words or grammar mas difficult to you and more.
  • And more
